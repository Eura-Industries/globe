1. Overview

Globe ("we", "us", or "our") is a collaborative travel planning tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Globe you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign up we collect your email address, display name, and (if you use Google or GitHub sign-in) a profile photo URL provided by that provider. We do not store your third-party passwords.

Travel board data

Any content you add to a board — text notes, place names, flight details, route waypoints, budget figures, uploaded files, and board names — is stored in our Firebase database and associated with your account.

Usage data

We collect anonymous usage events (page views, feature interactions) through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the product. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events.

Presence & collaboration data

While you have a board open, your cursor position and online status are broadcast in real-time to other collaborators on the same board via Firebase Realtime Database. This data is ephemeral and is removed when you close the board.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and sync your travel boards across devices and collaborators.
  • To authenticate you and protect your account.
  • To send you transactional emails (e.g. board invitation notifications).
  • To improve Globe's features based on aggregate, anonymised usage data.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Globe uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, file storage, and analytics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — place search and map tiles. Queries are sent to Nominatim's public API; no personal data is attached. Subject to the OSM Foundation Privacy Policy.
  • OSRM (Project-OSRM) — route calculations. Only geocoordinates are sent; no account data is transmitted.
  • Amadeus for Developers — optional flight search. Queries contain only airport codes and dates; no personal data is sent.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (EU/US regions). We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only you and your invited collaborators can read or modify your boards. Traffic between your browser and our services is encrypted with TLS.

6. Data Retention

Your boards and account data are retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual boards at any time from the dashboard. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Port your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact hello@eura.dev.

8. Cookies

Globe uses only essential cookies required for authentication (Firebase Auth session tokens). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Globe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Globe after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@eura.dev.
